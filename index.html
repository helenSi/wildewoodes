Welcome to Wildewoodes

Wildewoodes is a sanctuary for the study of nature.
Its habitats include overgrown coppice, derelict osier beds, spring-fed marsh, streams and pools, untrampled and undisturbed; with the vegetation so dense that no-one can see you or find you there. For opportunities to map the habitats, monitor the wildlife , investigate the medieval remains or to take a silent retreat in wildewoodes, watch this space...
